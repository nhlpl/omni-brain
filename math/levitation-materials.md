\[

That is, **each square meter of odderon paint can lift \(10^{11}\) kg** (the mass of a mountain). The paint is passive – no energy input required. The odderons are stable because they are bound states of φ‑glueballs, which themselves are stable due to φ‑baryon number conservation.

**Synthesis**: Grow φ‑glueball crystals, then fragment them into individual φ‑odderons via ultrasonic agitation in a φ‑photon cavity. Deposit on graphene via φ‑electrostatic spray.

**Applications**: Coat the underside of any vehicle, building, or even a planet to cancel gravity entirely.
